Card Game
4-8 players w/o 2 player option
Teams of two players compete for seating passengers on a train at their place, according to the card drawn. One team member tries to guide the other through gestures and calling the passenger's name. the other team member will then switch passengers' positions on a board. The first team who manages getting each passenger on his seat cries "Tokyo Train" and takes the card. The first team who gets 7 cards wins the game,
ALso called LOCO MOTIVE

RATING: 7
A simplified version of Hysterycoach. Better? Not really... For one, it seems a bit too easy to complete sometimes (I would have hoped than optimizing the solution would be a factor, but it seems that frenzy speed is more important). Maybe this would be solved in the harder version?

My biggest issue, though, is how it is just impossible to play for me: this game is not simply colorblind unfriendly, it is downright colorblind hateful! Three of the 6 colors are too similar for me, especially since you usually have low-lighting on the card (which stands upright). Plus, the colors on the character cards are "shaded" so they don't match perfectly the colored discs on your card. It would have been so simple to use some logos or a small version of the character face, or whatever... :(

We played several rounds of Do De Li Do on Christmas and had a blast. I can't remember to have laughed so hard for a long time. xD

The game is pretty straightforward and simple. Play a card face-upon one of three discard piles (you don't choose, you play on them clockwise) and then name what's the most prominent feature on the table: either one of the animals (crocodil, camel, penguin, tortoise or flamingo) or one of the five colors (white, blue, pink, green, yellow). If two features are equal on the table (e.g. two penguins and two blues), you have to say "Do De Li Do". Additionally, for each tortoise that's on the table, you have to say "Öh" (at least in the german version) before naming the prominent feature. If you make a mistake (or if someone plays a crocodile and you are the slowest to slap on the card), you take every card that's lying on the table, same goes if you take too long on your turn. First player to get rid of all his cards is the winner.

I guess you'll loose badly against children, who tend to be really good at these kind of games.We played it with six adults and some wine and it was hilarious. Either you make silly mistakes or you just sound silly with two tortoises on the table saying "Öh öh tortoise" or something. It's quick, easy to teach and simply great fun!

I give it 8 out of 10.

RATING: 6.8
To say that this game is a 3D version of Tetris would be an oversimplification and erroneous. Instead, the objective of Rumis is to have the most cube surfaces visible from the top by the end of the game. This is done by interlocking pieces with opponents until all pieces are played.

A wonderful abstract game, Rumis surprised me with how competitive games can get.

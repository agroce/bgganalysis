I really enjoy Cash 'n' Guns and from a rules readthrough, Repos has done a good (but somewhat more complicated) 'live' version of the original game. This time, foam guns are eschewed for loot cards with different weapons and abilities printed behind the monetary value. The object of the game is pretty much similar. Two teams get distributed weapons/armour by a Godfather. They then have to move around the room, stop when the whistle (yes, there's a whistle!) is blown by the Godfather and point their 'weapon' at an opponent. The usual chicken-out phase happens before weapons are effected in order. After 5 rounds, most money wins. Oh, anyone who's in the hospital (hurt) at the end of the game pays $25k. Team with most money wins. Should be a hit with large groups and at conventions. SIGN ME UP!! BANG! BANG! BANG!!

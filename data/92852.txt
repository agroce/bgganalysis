RATING: 7
I think I understand why Northstar made this game, because the original had questions that could (with the right crowd) lead to answers that were quite inappropriate.  So here they just toned down the questions, focusing on topics that are a little more family-friendly, and put some cute meeple artwork on the cards.  Overall the game is the same as the original, and that's a really good thing.  However it falls short because of one simple problem...fewer players.  If anything I want to play Say Anything with [i]more[/i] people, not less.  If this version played with the same number of players I would rate it as highly as the original, but as it stands I have to knock it down a few pegs.

RATING: 6.5
Fast playing, fun and cooperative. Clever mechanism that links storytelling with basic dexterity tests (throw a card in the box, build a cards castle, keep a card on top of your head). The game is not to be taken too seriously, but there is enough thematic flavour, and you are thrilled to play quickly and shout at each other as in the real thing (the internet audio series).
Also, players take turns being narrator, and one can try to roleplay his character (just for fun, no game impact).

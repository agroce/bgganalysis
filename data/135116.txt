RATING: 6
12-10-2014. Rated after 1.5 play. Interesting cardgame in which you try to collect 6 different warriors face-up in front of you. You start with 2 warriors in front of you.

On your turn, you flip the top card of the deck. This is the competition that you need to 'beat' this round (card has a rule on it (for example odd cards / lower than 3). You do this by playing a 'wandering hero', a card that fits the criterium by your challenge. 

The wandering hero goes to your neighbour, who may show you and discard a card from his hand (repeatedly) and ask if the wandering hero is the card they showed you.  This helps them deduce if they can use the card or not. If they take the card, they add it to their collection. If they already have the same hero in front of them both are discarded! If no one wants the wandering hero it ends up in your collection and you better make sure you don't have the card in front of you :P.

The warriors in front of you may be used once (and tapped) to use a special ability that is printed on them (draw 2 cards, look at the wandering hero etc).

The game is ok.. it's interesting but lacks depth and bluffing of other hidden information games. Also, the whole show a card mechanism slows the game down.

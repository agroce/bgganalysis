RATING: 6
A clever game that combines an economic engine, area control, and hidden victory conditions in a pretty short amount of time.

Players win in one of two ways: by reaching 20 points, or fulfilling the hidden recipe requirements dealt out at the beginning of the game. To achieve either, one must purchase "houses" on the board. If you own all houses in a "district," you receive a bonus of income, points, or a special power. The game starts out slowly, then accelerates as people own more districts which give them more money with which to buy more.

The theme is completely irrelevant to the game, but it makes the colors nicer. The board is made of eight different pieces that are configured randomly every time, giving it some variability. It's a good game, and well worth the meager investment.

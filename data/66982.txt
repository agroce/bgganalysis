RATING: 6
I like the boomerang bidding aspect. I enjoy that you get a bonus for leaving at the right point and judging when others are going to leave so that you can maximise your gains. I also enjoy that running out of boomerangs is deadly. However, I'm not sure about the blindly choosing what you're going after part. Certainly, there is a certain level of skill there that was evidenced in our second play, but I'm not sure it's enough. It can also be a bit dull when it works out that we all have our own animals and there's no real fighting. 

It also seems like it is a bit slower and more complicated than I want, like the rules are overcomplicating and moving us away from the most entertaining mechanism, which is the boomerang bidding. I may have to give No Thanks another try.

The downside is that I am pretty sure the cards from Big Taboo are much more poorly designed than the original game. For instance, there is a card named "CD-ROM" where "CD" is taboo, which is moronic since you can't say a part of the word anyway. Other example is one card that is "Artificial Turf" because the creators didn't want to use the trademarked Astroturf. Puh-leaze.

The new character is the Inquisitor, who may replace the Ambassador (the game still plays with 5 characters).

Inquisitor : either choice
- pick one card from the deck, having 3 in hand, return one card to the deck and shuffle
OR
- select a player who choose one of his cards to show to the Inquisitor ; then the Inquisitor decides to give it back to the player, or have him return the card to the deck, shuffle and pick a replacement card from the deck.

Play with 7 or 8 :
use 20 cards, 4 of each of the 5 characters chosen (remember : Ambassador or Inquisitor)

Play with 2 :
- 15 cards in play (3 of each of the 5 characters chosen)
- 3 packs of 5 cards, one pack for each player
- from the 3rd pack, each player receive one random card
- from their own deck of 5 cards, each player now choose one card to keep (the 4 other cards are not used for the game)
- each player has 2 cards, one random, and one he has chosen from 5

The deck is only made with the 3 leftover cards from the 3rd deck.

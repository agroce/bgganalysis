A simple filler game where players speak like cavemen. It is a memory game in the sense that one player will read a card and then say Ha, then the next player must remember the first card, say the new card and end with Ha. So you get sentences like Ooga Booga Ha, followed by Ooga Booga Cha Ha. Silly game that is fun for a quick round.

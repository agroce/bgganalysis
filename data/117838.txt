RATING: 6.5
NEW: Now that I have been collecting/playing board games for about a year, I can finally pin point what it is that I don't actually like about this game. It has a high potential to get too complicated for what it is. Sure, it's portable.  And sure, the rules are easy to explain. But once more rules get added, people who like simple card games have to be guided and reminded far to often for this to be enjoyable. It feels like it's supposed to be great for your average dinner party group, but that feeling wears off quickly as the game progresses. Cool mechanic. I wouldn't lower my score because this is still a solid game. But the theme and gameplay are not appealing to my more experienced group and it escalates quickly in complexity with typical non-gamers which leaves me doing far too much explaining and babying.

This game is fun.  It's not great, but it's good family fun.  It is a hectic game with a lot of time and energy spent keeping the rules and goals straight.  I suspect it gets better when played with people who do not need to have everything explained every turn.

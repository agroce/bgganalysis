RATING: 7
I enjoy solo logic puzzles and this is a great one.  Very nice chunky wooden pieces and a board that can be re-sized for different challenges.  The booklet that comes with the game contains a large amount of puzzles with varying difficulty.  This is a game that my kids will pick up and play at times, and it's great for introducing them to some spatial reasoning.  The included small cubes make things kid-friendly so they can solve the puzzles without becoming overly frustrated.  All in all a great puzzler (and the 2 player game version looks interesting, though I haven't tried yet) when you're in the mood for that.  Note:  the booklet does not contain solutions for the puzzles.

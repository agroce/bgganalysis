[Abacus 2004 edition] {Kid, beers&pretzels; reaction, set collection, real-time; optimal 4-6p} FK is an excellent children game. It can be used to train reflex action, colour matching and simple arithmetic. It's fun for adults as well. Recommended as a party reaction game.

FK is a unique because there are several targets on the table, while many other games have only one target when an event is triggered.

It's hilarious when most players hit the same card while a player hits another card slowly. It's also interesting to see players hit the wrong cards or hit the least value card very quickly! It's not bad if a player acts slowly sometimes because he can still pick up cards because other may block themselves in the same cards.

RATING: 7.7
Fun and educational to kids. Fun for adults as well. Streamlined gameplay. Funny when you see someone trying to take a low score fly rather a high score one. Hilarious when you see everyone hitting on the same fly (or hands then).
Truely a good game for 2 to 8 players.
I prefer to following the strict rules, such that you take nothing after hitting on a hand (players used to change mind if play casually). This offers more tension and tactics, and importantly allow slower gamers can catch up from choosing a un-hit fly.

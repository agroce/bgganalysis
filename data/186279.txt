Initial Rating: 6.0 (December 2015)

Fun outdoor dexterity game to play with the family or in a relaxed setting. However this is not my kind of game. 

The rules are very easy, you can explain them under 2 minutes and the game plays very fast as well. It can support a lot of players, but I prefer to play with 6 at most.

It features player elimination which happened to some friends when playing. I´m not sure I like this.

Molkky looks great when it is new, but it gets dirty on the first play and, I’m sad to say, I’m bothered a lot by this.

Bottom line, I think I will sell my copy of the game but I could play it from time to time.

While its probably more an activity than a game, it is a superb one! I have played it many many times and it never fell flat. Its one of the view games were the players start to laugh before they have done anything at all. Zu Beginn der Apokalypse fallen Zeugen Jehovas und Wartezimmerliteratur vom Himmel!

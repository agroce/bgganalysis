RATING: 6
This comment will mostly be a comparison to the original game; if you want to learn more about the game itself (such as how mean it can be!), read that comment. 

I like the original Beasty Bar quite a bit, so I had to get this one as well as I thought it would be nice to mix up the animals. However, while a couple of the animals have cool abilities which I prefer to the base game animals (such as the Peacock and the Dog), I overall find this standalone game to have too many "weird" abilities or just abilities that are less interesting than those in the original game. And some of them are just too fiddly such as the Vulture. And even though I have a love/hate-relationship with the Seal from the original game, it's just too important to leave out I think.

So, overall I much prefer the original game, but would play this from time to time or perhaps mix some of the animals from each game.

The game gives us more of the same really.  I love the base game and this is a welcome extra set of 12 animals, but it isn’t doing anything different, apart from the new animal-skill of sending animals to the back of the line.

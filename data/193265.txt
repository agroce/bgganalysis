RATING: 6.75
A good adaptation of Port Royal into an even lighter game. The character cards are very different, and make for a much shorter game. It's also much simpler to learn for very young children.

Advertised as a travel version, but the box and insert are extremely impractical for that purpose. The game material consists in 60 cards, nothing more, nothing less, which are loose in the oversized 18.5cm x 11cm x 3.5cm box. I highly recommend printing a deck-sized box to accommodate the cards.

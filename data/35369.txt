RATING: 7
It's Fluxx, so it's fun, but this was actually not as good as I was expecting it to be. While there seems to be more strategy behind what the cards do in the game play, it seems to take away from the random fun that Fluxx is - almost putting the way the theme is handled in opposition to the core game.

It felt like the game would have benefited from a greater number of human creepers. They just didn't seem to come up often enough.

I also felt like certain card actions that you could perform were kind of pointless - such as being able to prevent people from stealing your human creepers. And even if there's one goal or new rule card that benefits from you keeping your creepers, it just doesn't feel like enough to warrant the protection effect. This gave the impression that there would be or needed to be an expansion.

This one will probably be last in line in which version of Fluxx I play in the future.

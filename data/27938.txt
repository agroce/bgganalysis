RATING: 5
A checkers variant with attractive "native art" style turtles.  A jumped piece is flipped onto its back and temporarily neutralized, then the next time someone jumps it, it joins their side.  A practical problem is that the turtle backs are round, so they rock inconveniently when on their back, and the turtles are a bit too large for the hexes, so crowded areas of the board become cumbersome.  Possibly a good checkers variant, but the practical issues turned us off.

RATING: 7
I've been interested in this game for awhile, being both a word game fan and a Sackson fan. But I never really pulled the trigger as it gets somewhat middling reviews.  Glad I found a thrift copy and was finally able to try it out! BuyWord definitely does something different, but it is pleasant and more thinky than word games usually are (beyond the memory retrieval portion).  The secret is to buy letters cheap but then find yourself in the position to sell large words off at a profit.  This is managed by squaring the value of the tiles bought and squaring the price of the words sold.  As such, the larger the word - the better the payoff, so it's always worth holding off and trying to make the largest word possible (which gets around the inevitable 2-letter word issues with Scrabble).  The strategy becomes quite apparent by the end and you are then relying on your word skills for big payouts.  But it's fun to consider what to buy and when to sell, and there's a bit of push-your-luck in this one which I enjoy.  Overall, this is a very worthy addition to the small coterie of word games I own.

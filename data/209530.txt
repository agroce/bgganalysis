Good card game which is easy to learn. It's very basic though, and I think the spy cards are quite over powered which usually makes both players use them, which then leads to the games being just about luck and not much strategy. You build your own deck and many cards are really useless. The artwork is really lovely. I don't understand the point of the "bovine defense force" and it sometimes feels like some rules are missing from the rule book. A must have if love the witcher games, but be aware! Almost all decks on ebay are fake, the only real decks come with the physical expansion copies of the witcher 3 games.

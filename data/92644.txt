RATING: 8
Most excellent for our 6-8 year olds. They even play completely without any supervisiona and with non-German players (they simply explain the concept to them). I am really astonished how much it can capture the kids. It's challenging on difficult level, so the kids usely take the 2nd level when playing. When parents/adults are joining in, you take the 3rd level (most difficult).

The annoying long sentences can be shortened by simply pressing the buttons. So far, after three weeks of non-stop playing, the batteries still work and the plastic computer is not showing signs of wear and tear. I fear it will be the first game to be played to its physical death. Then we buy another one.

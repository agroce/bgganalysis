RATING: 6
[thing=134150][/thing] is a simple dexterity game, much similar to [thing=37196][/thing]: players push pieces that slide by the playing area, trying to get as many of them as close to the goal as possible, in order to score more of them. Whoever has the most point at the end of 8 rounds (though we usually only play 4 rounds), will be the winner.

It is simple as a game can get, with basically no complexity, besides managing the direction and strenght of the push. Maybe skilled players can use strategy in positioning and ricochet, but I only toss the pieces and hope for the best.

[thing=134150][/thing] is super easy to explain and to play, working for people of any age (but the younglings may have difficult in tossing the pieces properly). It is reasonable compact, the set up takes less than 30 seconds and it is likely entertain almost everyone, working for gamers and non-gamers.

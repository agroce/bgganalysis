This is a light party game which is a great Ice Breaker!  Can play up to 12-players, and would recommend a minimum of 5 or 6.

In a nutshell, one player reads one of 6 (?) questions one one of the cards, and everyone "votes" via their Yes/No card, which they place in the voting box, then everyone guesses how many "Yes" can be found.

Everyone who guessed exactly the number of yeses scores 2 points.  If there are no exact guess(es), then everyone who is within "1" of the number of Yesses scores 1 point.

First player to 10 points is the winner.

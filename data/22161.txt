RATING: 10
If you own WestFront, the WestFront -> WestFront II upgrade is less compelling than EF -> EFII if you don't have aspirations to play campaign games. The WF map has fewer issues than the EF map (which benefits from the added clarity and extension to the East in EFII). The WFII main map now has Spain, which can come into play if the Western Allies are insane, but you still need EuroFront II to play the Spanish Civil War or add in the Norwegian possibilities. I like the upgrade, but I also play EuroFront from time to time. The combined package (WFII   EuFII) is definitely substantially superior to WF   MF   EuF, though, and I think serious fans will want to upgrade.

RATING: 8
Excellent memory game for kids with a push your luck element.  Also shakes up the scoring with some items that require only 1 tile to collect, others 2, 3 or 4 tiles.  Beware the dragon who will force you to return your treasure ... unless you're able to capture the most dragons, in which case you receive a scoring bonus at the end.
Currently my kids' favorite memory game.  Great components (very thick tiles) too!
Edit:  Tried a house rule for our 2p games where any player who gets 10 of the 12 dragons automatically wins.  This works well for us b/c my 5 y.o. daughter tries to collect the dragon bonus (the 3 spider cards) in every game she plays.  This way, I have to make sure I don't let her run away with the dragons.

An elegant, fresh take on the classic memory game. Simple enough to explain to a four-year-old (though as with adults it may take a round or two for everything to sink in), but with just enough push-your-luck strategizing to keep it interesting for the adults. Great art on chunky tiles. Takes a bit of table space to play (it can't be played on a plane or train, say), but is otherwise highly transportable.
Update: My eldest loved it but has moved on, and his younger brother never seemed to request it, so it has moved on to other gaming pastures.

RATING: 7.5

Schatz der Drachen er et fornøjeligt børnespil - også selvom man kun spiller det med voksne. Og så er der endda tale om en Memory-variant, hvilket ellers kan føles som hårdt arbejde! I Schatz der Drachen kan man enten samle serier af gode ting (legetøj, kandelabre, kufferter etc.), hvor serierne består af et varierende antal brikker eller man kan samle uhyrer, som man kan samle så mange af, man er i stand til. Er man først begyndt at samle den ene type brikker (gode ting hhv. uhyrer), så afbrydes ens tur straks, hvis man kommer til at samle en ting op fra den modsatte kategori. Til gengæld kan man samle så mange par indenfor den enkelte kategori, som man kan og / eller er i stand til. Ligegyldigt om man samler på den ene eller den anden type af brikker, må man altid samtidigt samle diamantringe op. Samler man til gengæld en edderkop slutter ens tur altid straks. Edderkoppen må man til gengæld lægge tilbage lige hvor man har lyst.

RATING: 5
[Winning Moves 'Schatz der Drachen' 2003 edition] {Kid; memory, luck-pressing} SdD is a cute memory game for children. It is similar to Memory but it incorporates a luck-pressing element to the game.  It does not require very strong memory because players only need to remember 7 kinds of items.A game runs faster, around 15 minutes per game.  It is quite fun; esp. when you try your luck to open more tiles but encounter either dragon / spider.  However, the end game is a bit anti-climax because player only needs to remember the exact location of the 3 spiders after all dragons have been collected - a player can just collect everything remaining on the board.

The "characters" edition of a super fun party game.

The clever idea here is the three rounds.

First round is Taboo without any forbidden word. Second round is Taboo where one can use only a single word. Third round is mimes.

So, where is the cleverness? You always use the same subset of 30 cards, so the second and third rounds are an exercise in synthesis and memory!

For what it is, a great funny game! With the right crowd of social oriented (alcohol fueled…) casual gamers, can be a blast!

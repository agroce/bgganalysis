Initial Rating: 6.0 (May 2014)

I don’t really like racing games, still 1911 is one of the tolerable ones for me. The game plays quickly and that is its best quality. If the game was longer I would rate it much lower. It has lots of uncontrollable luck and “take that” moments but as the game flows so quickly I don’t mind much about the luck. If both players focus on hurting each other, they will both lose (frozen), a good feature.

The game allows for some clever moves (mostly from the multiple card uses) but most of the time, the decisions are obvious, so it is very shallow and simple for my taste. 

Ok art and components although I dislike the 2 piece board. It moves very easily.

Love that the game come with some expansion ready in the base game, perhaps one of them can make it better for me. Cool theme.

I suspect that if I play it often, its rating will plunge.

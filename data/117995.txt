RATING: 8
I rated this a 8 for playing with a mixed group of children and adults, maybe a 9 for just a group of kids, for adults alone, it might be fun as a way to select first player or something.... This game is fast paced (2-3 minutes per round, a single round could be a complete game, or you can keep going).  My 3 year old and 6 year old can play and hold their own as long as the adults aren't being ultra competitive.  

Most kids games are almost entirely luck based (Candyland, Chutes & ladders, etc) and the adult feels like a trained monkey could play so its not much fun for the adult.  Or you try to play a more complex game, which is usually too complex for kids to win unless the adult throws the game.  This game is about matching pictures, all that matters is how fast do your eyes, brain, mouth and hands work together.  In general adults are probably better at it, but not by such a margin that the kids aren't in the game.    

I don't have to compromise my integrity (too much) to give my kids a fair chance to win, they love it, and even adults can get into the spirit of the game.

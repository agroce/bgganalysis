RATING: 8.5
Another great game from the Cry Havoc series. The Coast map is very tactically interesting and the ship rules are a nice addition. I like the players notes section in each scenario. I don't think the counters are quite at attractive as Siege or Cry Havoc, but still nice. The rules for the Eurogames edition are available at cryhavocgames.net and are better than the Standard Games edition. I recommend those. To me this game really captures the "feel" of the Dark Ages. Over all a great game. I rated this game lower than Cry Havoc and Siege primarily because I prefer the Middle Ages to the Dark Ages, and to me the counters are not quite as attractive
